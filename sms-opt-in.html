How to Sign Up

For Yard Staff

  1. Onboarding: When your organization administrator adds you to the YardHawk system, they will enter your mobile phone number
  2. Automatic Opt-In: By providing your number during onboarding, you consent to receive operational SMS notifications
  3. Confirmation: You'll receive a welcome SMS confirming your enrollment
  4. Start Receiving: Begin getting PullTag notifications when equipment needs to be gathered

For Rental Customers

  1. Contract Setup: When you rent equipment, provide your mobile phone number for return reminders
  2. Explicit Consent: Your rental company will ask if you'd like to receive SMS reminders (optional)
  3. First Reminder: You'll receive a text reminder before your rental is due back

Manual Opt-In (Alternative Method)

  1. Text START to our toll-free number: [Number will be assigned]
  2. You'll receive a confirmation message
  3. Provide your organization name and role when prompted

Message Frequency

PullTag (Yard Staff): Variable frequency based on order volume. Typically 5-20 messages per day during peak operations.

ReturnPing (Customers): 1-2 messages per rental contract. First reminder 24 hours before due time, possible follow-up if overdue.

Important: Messages are transactional and operational only. We do not send marketing or promotional SMS.

Opt-Out Anytime

You can stop receiving messages at any time:

  • Text STOP: Reply STOP to any message from YardHawk
  • Contact Admin: Ask your organization administrator to disable SMS for your account
  • Email Support: Contact support@betaranch.com

After opting out, you'll receive one final confirmation message, then no further messages unless you choose to opt back in.
